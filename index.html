Meet DreiWhite

Founder & Server Owner of SolanaMC

His journey began not with a massive team or a budget, but with a passion to create something meaningful. What started as a small server experiment turned into a full-fledged community loved by players for its fairness, creativity, and active vibe.

From plugin configuration to in-game event hosting, DreiWhite wears many hats. He's known for being present, listening to feedback, and responding to players — not with bots or prewritten replies, but with honest conversations and real improvements. His priority has always been to create a Minecraft experience that feels fresh yet familiar, where every player feels seen and valued.

The server's focus on fair gameplay, zero pay-to-win mechanics, and an evolving world of community-led features reflects his belief that a great server isn’t just about performance or plugins — it’s about the people who play on it. Under DreiWhite’s leadership, SolanaMC has grown into a home for builders, adventurers, redstone engineers, and casual players alike.

He continues to push updates, organize creative events, and implement thoughtful mechanics that give players something to look forward to every time they log in. Whether it's enhancing survival gameplay or building an in-server economy that feels natural and player-driven, his eye for balance and dedication to fun is clear in every block placed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about playing and enjoying SolanaMC.

How do I join SolanaMC?

Launch Minecraft Java Edition, go to Multiplayer, click “Add Server” and use our IP: play.solanamc.net.

Is SolanaMC cracked or premium?

SolanaMC supports both cracked and premium accounts, so everyone is welcome to join!

What game modes are available?

We offer unique modes like Survival SMP, Skyblock, and seasonal events. More features are coming soon!

Do I need mods to play?

No mods are required! Just join with vanilla Minecraft. However, we recommend using Optifine for better performance.

Is there a Discord server?

Yes! Join our Discord community to meet other players, get updates, and participate in events.

Can I apply for staff?

Yes! Keep an eye on our Discord or website announcements for staff applications.
